Bhima Showroom, Malleswaram

The Bhima Showroom in Malleswaram, Bengaluru, is a distinguished commercial project completed in 2021 by Blendinkos Architects, Kollam. Spanning 4,960 sq. ft., the design integrates modern retail architecture with refined interior aesthetics. The layout is thoughtfully planned to optimize customer flow and enhance jewellery display visibility. Strategic lighting design creates a luxurious and inviting shopping atmosphere. High-quality finishes reinforce the brand’s premium identity and visual appeal. Spatial zoning ensures efficient operations for staff while maintaining a seamless customer journey. Clean lines and balanced proportions bring clarity and elegance to the interior spaces. Durable materials support long-term performance in a high-traffic retail environment. The showroom emphasizes comfort and accessibility for all visitors. This project stands as a refined example of contemporary commercial showroom design in Bengaluru.
