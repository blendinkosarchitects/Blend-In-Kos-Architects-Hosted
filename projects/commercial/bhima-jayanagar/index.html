Bhima Showroom Jayanagar

The Bhima Showroom in Jayanagar, Bengaluru, is a landmark commercial project completed in 2020 by Blendinkos Architects, Kollam. Spanning 8,851 sq. ft., the showroom blends modern retail design with efficient spatial planning. The layout is carefully crafted to ensure seamless customer circulation and clear product visibility. A strong brand identity is reinforced through premium materials and refined architectural detailing. Strategic lighting enhances key display areas and creates a sophisticated shopping atmosphere. The interior palette balances elegance with durability for long-term commercial use. Functional zoning supports smooth staff operations and an intuitive customer journey. Open spaces promote comfort and accessibility throughout the showroom. Clean lines and cohesive finishes bring visual harmony to the interiors. This project reflects a commitment to delivering high-quality commercial architecture in Bengaluru.
