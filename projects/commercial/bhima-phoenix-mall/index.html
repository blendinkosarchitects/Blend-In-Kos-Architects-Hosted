Bhima Showroom, Phoenix Mall

The Bhima Showroom at Phoenix Mall is a compact yet elegant commercial project completed in 2018 by Blendinkos Architects, Kollam. Spanning 1,600 sq. ft., the design demonstrates how efficient planning can create a luxurious retail experience within a limited footprint. The layout is crafted to maximize product visibility and smooth customer circulation. Refined material selections enhance the brand’s premium identity and visual appeal. Carefully designed lighting highlights jewellery collections and key display areas. Clean lines and a sleek interior palette bring a contemporary character to the space. Functional zoning supports efficient staff operations and customer service. Open spatial planning ensures a seamless and comfortable shopping journey. Durable finishes provide long-term performance in a high-traffic mall environment. This project stands as a refined example of boutique showroom design within a commercial mall setting.
