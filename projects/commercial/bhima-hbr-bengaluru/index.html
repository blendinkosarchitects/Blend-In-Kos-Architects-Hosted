Bhima Showroom – HBR, Bengaluru

The Bhima Showroom in HBR, Bengaluru, is a premium commercial project completed in 2023 by Blendinkos Architects, Kollam. Spanning 8,400 sq. ft., the showroom blends modern retail architecture with a strong focus on customer experience. The layout is thoughtfully planned to enhance circulation and clear product visibility. Brand identity is reinforced through refined materials and elegant architectural detailing. Strategic lighting highlights key display areas and creates a warm, inviting shopping atmosphere. The interior design balances sophistication with durability for long-term commercial performance. Functional zoning ensures efficient operations for staff while maintaining a smooth customer journey. Open spatial planning promotes comfort and accessibility throughout the showroom. Clean lines and a cohesive color palette add visual clarity and harmony. This project stands as a benchmark for high-impact commercial showroom design in Bengaluru.
