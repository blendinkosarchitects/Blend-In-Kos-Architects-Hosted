Bhima Showroom, Rajajinagar, Bengaluru

The Bhima Showroom in Rajajinagar, Bengaluru, is a flagship commercial project completed in 2022 by Blendinkos Architects, Kollam. Spanning 10,500 sq. ft., the showroom combines luxury design with functional retail planning. The layout is carefully structured to optimize customer circulation and enhance product visibility. Premium material finishes and elegant detailing reinforce the brand’s luxury identity. Strategic lighting highlights key display areas, creating an inviting shopping atmosphere. Functional zoning supports smooth staff operations while maintaining a seamless customer experience. Open spatial planning promotes comfort, accessibility, and a clutter-free environment. Clean lines and a cohesive color palette create visual harmony throughout the interior. Durable materials ensure long-term performance in a high-traffic commercial space. This project exemplifies contemporary large-scale commercial showroom design in Bengaluru.
